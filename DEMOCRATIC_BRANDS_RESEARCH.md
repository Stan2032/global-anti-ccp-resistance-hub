# Democratic Country Brands - Expanded Research

## Criteria:
1. **Manufactured in democratic countries** - USA, EU, UK, Japan, S. Korea, Taiwan, Canada, Australia, NZ
2. **Transparent supply chains** - Company discloses manufacturing locations
3. **Accountable governance** - Subject to democratic oversight and labor laws
4. **No China manufacturing** - Zero production in authoritarian regimes

## IMPORTANT NOTES:

### Verification Required:
Many brands have **mixed manufacturing**. Always check:
- Specific product lines
- "Made in" labels
- Company websites for manufacturing locations
- Recent changes in production locations

### Democratic Countries Prioritized:
1. **EU** - Strong labor laws, democratic oversight
2. **UK** - Post-Brexit but still democratic with accountability
3. **Japan** - High quality standards, transparent
4. **South Korea** - Democratic, strong labor protections
5. **Taiwan** - Democratic, tech manufacturing hub
6. **Canada, Australia, NZ** - Democratic with strong labor laws

### Avoid:
- ❌ China (authoritarian, forced labor concerns)
- ❌ Russia (authoritarian, war aggressor)
- ❌ Belarus (authoritarian, Russia ally)
- ❌ Myanmar (military dictatorship)
- ❌ North Korea (totalitarian)
- ❌ Turkmenistan, Uzbekistan (authoritarian)

### Caution (Verify Labor Practices):
- ⚠️ Vietnam (communist but improving labor standards)
- ⚠️ Bangladesh (democratic but weak labor enforcement)
- ⚠️ Turkey (democratic backsliding)
- ⚠️ Thailand (military influence)
- ⚠️ Cambodia (authoritarian tendencies)

### Generally Acceptable (Democratic with Labor Protections):
- ✅ Portugal, Spain, Italy (EU labor laws)
- ✅ Poland, Czech Republic (EU labor laws)
- ✅ Mexico (USMCA labor protections)
- ✅ South Africa (democratic, labor protections)
- ✅ India (democratic, improving standards)
- ✅ Sri Lanka (democratic)

---

## Key Principle:

> **Support democracies that can be held accountable for labor practices and human rights violations**

This means prioritizing countries with:
1. **Free press** - Can expose abuses
2. **Independent judiciary** - Can prosecute violations
3. **Labor unions** - Can advocate for workers
4. **Democratic elections** - Can vote out bad actors
5. **International accountability** - Subject to EU/UN oversight

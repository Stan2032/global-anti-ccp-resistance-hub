## 🧠 MEMORABILITY SCIENCE APPLICATION

### Cognitive Psychology Principles

**The Von Restorff Effect (Isolation Effect):**
- Make key facts visually distinct
- Use unexpected colors, fonts, animations
- Break patterns to create memorable moments

**The Picture Superiority Effect:**
- Every major fact paired with striking visuals
- Infographics over text whenever possible
- Video demonstrations of propaganda techniques

**The Serial Position Effect:**
- Most important facts at beginning and end
- Middle content supports but doesn't carry key messages
- Repetition of crucial points throughout experience

**The Generation Effect:**
- Users discover facts through interaction, not passive reading
- "Aha!" moments through guided exploration
- Personal revelation rather than being told

### Memory Palace Integration

**Spatial Memory Utilization:**
- Virtual "rooms" for different propaganda categories
- Users navigate through physical spaces
- Each room contains memorable visual anchors
- Progressive unlocking creates exploration motivation

**Story-Based Fact Integration:**
- Facts embedded in compelling narratives
- Character-driven examples (real people affected)
- Plot twists that reveal propaganda techniques
- Emotional climaxes tied to key learning moments

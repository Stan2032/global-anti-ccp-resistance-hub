WE DEFEND THE HUMAN RIGHTS OF UYGHUR PEOPLE AND THE FREE WORLD BY EXPOSING AND CONFRONTING THE CHINESE GOVERNMENT'S GENOCIDE, AND EMPOWERING UYGHUR WOMEN AND YOUTH IN THE DIASPORA.

Campaign for Uyghurs (CFU) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit founded in 2017 to confront the ongoing genocide of the Uyghur people by the Chinese Communist Party (CCP). We work to expose the full scope of the CCP’s crimes, including mass internment, forced labor, digital surveillance, family separation, and the systematic erasure of Uyghur culture and religion, while defending the fundamental rights and dignity of the Uyghur people. Our mission centers on amplifying the voices of the diaspora, mobilizing public awareness, and driving meaningful international action.

Host an Event
Book Events

Hold a book event for the memoir Unbroken: One Uyghurs Fight for Freedom. This creates space for survivor-led storytelling, where audiences hear directly from Uyghurs about the human cost of the genocide and the resilience of the Uyghur people. 

Film Screenings

Host a screening of the documentary “In Search of My Sister” and hold a panel discussion with Uyghur activists and experts in order to educate your community about the Uyghur genocide. The news, data and evidence is there and is compelling, however, what is most likely to move the hearts and minds of people, is a personal story.
